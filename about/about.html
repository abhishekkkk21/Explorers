A good travel website provides a seamless and user-friendly experience, making it easy to search for and book flights, accommodations, and activities. It should offer a wide range of options, from budget-friendly choices to luxurious stays, and include detailed descriptions and reviews to help users make informed decisions. Interactive maps and personalized recommendations based on user preferences can enhance the planning process. The website should also feature a responsive design, ensuring it works well on both desktops and mobile devices.
